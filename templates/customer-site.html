Why Choose Ampoulex

Built for Pharmaceutical Excellence

We combine precision manufacturing with rigorous quality control to deliver packaging that meets the highest pharma standards.

ISO Certified Quality

Our manufacturing processes and products are certified to ISO 9001:2015 and comply with international pharmacopeial standards.

Nationwide Delivery

Fast, insured delivery across all major cities in Pakistan with proper cushioning and moisture protection for fragile ampoules.

Competitive Pricing

Volume-based pricing with tiered discounts for bulk orders. Flexible payment terms available for regular customers.

Rigorous QC Testing

Every batch undergoes hydrolytic resistance, breakage force, and dimensional tolerance testing before dispatch.

Custom Printing & White Label

Full printing service for your branding. Screen printing and self-adhesive labels available on both clear and amber glass.

Dedicated Support

A dedicated account manager for each client. From sample approval to batch delivery — we handle every step with you.
