Root Cause Analysis

Identify the root cause of a quality non-conformance using the 5-Why or Fishbone method.

  • Review batch production records
  • Check raw material COA/test results
  • Verify equipment calibration status
  • Review operator records & training
Start from a Complaint

Corrective Actions

Actions taken to eliminate the cause of a detected non-conformity to prevent recurrence.

  • Update SOPs / work instructions
  • Retraining of operators
  • Equipment repair or replacement
  • Supplier change / qualification
Review QC Parameters

Preventive Actions

Proactive actions to eliminate potential causes of non-conformities before they occur.

  • Risk assessment of processes
  • Scheduled preventive maintenance
  • Statistical process control (SPC)
  • Regular internal audits
Manage QC Parameters
